Ceremony Site

This outdoor venue is surrounded by majestic aspen trees. We strive to make your speacial day unforgetable and everything you want it to be. This romantic venue includes an area for a DJ/Band, hanging lightss, a runner for the bride to walk down, and and a pergola where you and your parenter will say your vows.

Capacity: 150 people Ceremony: Is outdoors but can be moved into the Golden Barn if there is bad weather Reception: Will be held in the Golden Barn Dressing suites available on site
